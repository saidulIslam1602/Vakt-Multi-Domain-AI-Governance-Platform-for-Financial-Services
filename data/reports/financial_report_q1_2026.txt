MANAGEMENT COMMENTARY

Q1 2026 demonstrates strong top-line momentum with 31.6% YoY revenue growth
driven primarily by continued SaaS subscription expansion (+35.6%). The
company onboarded 12 new enterprise customers in the quarter.

Operating cost growth of 23.1% was intentional, reflecting accelerated
investment in cloud infrastructure (+44.6%) and go-to-market (+48.8%) to
support the strategic growth roadmap approved by the Board in December 2025.

EBITDA margin of 19.5% is in line with FY2026 budget targets (20% for Q1).

Key risks for Q2 2026:
  1. Cloud infrastructure costs expected to increase NOK 150–200k in Q2
     as new Azure region migration completes.
  2. Renewal pipeline for Q2 represents NOK 4.2M ARR — retention team engaged.
  3. Office lease renewal (Oslo HQ) effective 2026-07-01 adds NOK 78k
     to quarterly rent cost from Q3 onwards.
