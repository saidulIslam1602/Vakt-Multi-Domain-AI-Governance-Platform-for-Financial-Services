\section{Executive Summary}

Allergo Nordic AS delivered a strong Q1 2026, with revenue of \textbf{NOK 12.4M} (+19\% YoY),
ahead of budget by 3.5\%. EBITDA margin reached \textbf{44.2\%} (NOK 5.5M), supported by
disciplined cost management and the positive effect of the Equinor Technology Solutions contract
which commenced 1 January 2026.

Cash position remains healthy at NOK 28.7M (Q4 2025: NOK 24.1M). Accounts Receivable aging
improved: DSO of 27 days vs.\ 34 days in Q1 2025. Two strategic investments were approved by
the Board in March 2026: the Allergo Digital AI Platform (NOK 4.2M CapEx) and the Microsoft
Azure Enterprise Agreement renewal (NOK 1.06M).

\section{Outlook and Guidance}

\textbf{Q2 2026 Guidance:} Revenue NOK 13.2M -- 13.8M. EBITDA margin target 43\%--46\%.
Key drivers: Equinor full-quarter contribution, Aker BP implementation milestone payment,
and initial Allergo Digital revenue from pilot customers.

\textbf{FY2026 Reaffirmed:} Revenue NOK 52M (previously guided). EBITDA margin 42\%--44\%.
CapEx NOK 4.2M (Allergo Digital). The Board has approved a NOK 2.5M dividend to be paid Q3 2026.
